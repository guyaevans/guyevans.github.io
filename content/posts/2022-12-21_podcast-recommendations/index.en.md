- Darknet Diaries - Jack Rhysider
- Risky Business - Patrick Gray
- The Lazarus Hiest - Geoff White & Jean Lee - BBC Sounds 

## Crypto Scams

- The Missing Cryptoqueen - Jamie Bartlett - BBC Sounds
- Exit Scam - Threats Media

## Crime
