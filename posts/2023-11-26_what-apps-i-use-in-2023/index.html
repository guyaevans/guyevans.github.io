What Apps I use in 2023

Guy Evans
 2023-11-27  2023-11-27  226 words  2 minutes 
Contents

I saw this idea pop-up on BrainBaking and as I’m always interested in seeing what tools and apps other people are using I thought I should join in. So here is a selection of my favourite apps I use on a daily basis.

  • 🧑‍💻 Browser: Firefox & Safari on iOS - (Why you should dump Chrome)
  • 📧 Email: Gmail - Google Workspace & Mailcow (Personal) - Outlook - Office365 (Work)
  • ✅ Tasks: Obsidian - Planner/Projects - Notebook/PostIt Notes and a nice Rotring 600
  • 📰 RSS: Nextcloud News & NextNews iOS App
  • ☁️ Cloud Storage: selfhosted Nextcloud (Personal) - OneDrive (Work)
  • 🖼️ Photo Editing: Adobe Photoshop
  • 📆 Calendar: Apple Calendar with Google Calendar (Personal) & Outlook Calendar (Work)
  • 🎙️ Podcasts: Overcast
  • 🎵 Music: Spotify
  • 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Social: X - Instagram - Reddit
  • 📖 Reading: Amazon Kindle Paperwhite
  • 🔒 Passwords: VaultWarden (Selfhosted BitWarden)
  • 📓 Notes: Markdown in VSCode or Obsidian & Notebook & Pen
  • 📽️ Presentations: Marp & VSCode
  • 📝 Text Editor: Visual Code & Markdown
  • 💻 Terminal: iTerm (MacOS) - Terminal with WSL2 (Windows) - both with Oh My Zsh
  • 🏠 Home Automatation: Home Assistant

Got a favorite app you use everyday? Let us know!
